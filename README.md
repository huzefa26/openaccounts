# OpenAccounts

A personal accounting app that handles all your finances efficiently, with minimal data entry and automated data ingestions from statements and emails.

Your entire data lies on your Google account only. We do not have any backend servers.

The double-entry (sum of credits = sum of debits) concept of accounting is followed.
You can have multiple currencies, track your investments(not live tracking, just the funds you have put in them. You can set their current value to estimate your networth), track balances with your friends, view charts for spending and cashflow.

You can set your own chart of accounts - link them to a category, currency and a parent chart of account.
The base chart of accounts are pre-defined.
